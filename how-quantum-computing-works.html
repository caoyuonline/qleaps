How Does Quantum Computing Work?

This module will introduce you to the basic principles of quantum computing and provide an in-depth look into how quantum computing breaks through the limitations of classical computing.

To understand how quantum computing works, you first need to understand its core concepts:

  • Qubits Understand the basic unit of quantum computing and how it differs from classical bits.
  • Superposition Understand how qubits can represent multiple states at the same time.
  • Entanglement Understand the special relationship between qubits.
  • Quantum Gates Understand how quantum gates manipulate the state of qubits.
  • Quantum Algorithms Understand how quantum algorithms use quantum properties to achieve high-speed computing.
